About Us

Nurturing Excellence & Character

Agra Citizen School has been a beacon of educational excellence for over a decade. Our commitment to CBSE standards combined with holistic development ensures every student emerges as a confident, compassionate global citizen ready to face tomorrow's challenges.

We believe in fostering critical thinking, creativity, and character—values that define our institution and create lasting impact in our students' lives.
